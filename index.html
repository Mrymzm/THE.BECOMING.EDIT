About

The Becoming Edit Is Built on One Idea.

You can't think your way into a different life. You have to rewire it.

I'm Maryam — a Doctor of Physical Therapy who got tired of self-help that ignored the body entirely. The nervous system is where identity actually lives. Your patterns, your defaults, your reactions — they're not character flaws. They're physiology.

The Becoming Edit is where I break that down. Weekly essays, monthly journal prompts, and a community for women who are done with surface-level advice and ready to do the actual work — from the inside out.

8K+ on Threads. Weekly newsletter. Five-pillar journal series. All built around one question: Who are you actually becoming?
